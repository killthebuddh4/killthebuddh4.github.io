What's "kill the buddha"?

I asked ChatGPT, it gave me a pretty good answer:

"Kill the Buddha" is a phrase that comes from Zen Buddhism. It is often used to describe the idea of rejecting preconceived notions or ideas, even those that are considered sacred or fundamental to a particular belief system. The phrase is meant to encourage individuals to think for themselves and not be swayed by the beliefs of others, including religious or spiritual leaders. It is also a reminder that no one, not even the Buddha himself, is above criticism or scrutiny.
